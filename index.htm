Beginners Courses


Please contact Mike on the details below for up-to-date options and enrollment.

Starts: To Be Confirmed

Venue: To Be Confirmed

This is ‘ArcheryGB’s’ recommended ‘Beginners Course’ consisting of 6 sessions of up to 2hrs each aimed both at juniors aged from 8 to 15 and adults. While the objective is to have fun, emphasis will focus on safety, bearing in mind the bow is a weapon.

All Equipment will be provided for the duration of the Course.

At the end of the Course you will:

  • Receive a Certificate confirming that you have completed a Beginners Course in Target Archery, a requirement for joining most Archery Clubs.
  • Know and understand the Safety rules and Etiquette of the sport.
  • Be familiar with the Basic equipment used in Target Archery.
  • Be able to consistently hit a Target at 20 yards.
  • Aware of the Scoring systems used in Target Archery.
  • Know and understand the Basic Shooting Rules of Target Archery.
  • Have received information on other types of Archery and its History.
  • Have received advice on the selection of your own equipment.
  • Be able to conduct some simple maintenance on your Archery Equipment.
